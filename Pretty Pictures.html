Here are some photos from my time in South Africa


Johannesburg

Here is a photo of me at the Nelson Mandela statue in Johannesburg. I was in this country for about a week.
This was during my Christmas break, when it is summertime for them.

Johannesburg (continued)

My travelling class had the opportunity to visit what is called "The Lion's Den", where there are many lions housed. We took turns playing with the lion cubs.


  
  

Capetown

I had the opportunity to travel to Capetown after leaving Johannesburg. There we visited the Table Mountains and Robben Island where Nelson Mandela was imprisoned.
