What are we making: 2 Egg Breakfast and Sausage

by Dasia James

Growing up, I never knew how to cook. When I finally learned, the easiest thing for me to learn was eggs and sausage. So everytime I cooked breakfast for family I was responsible for the eggs and sausage.
I usually eat this meal as a quick breakfast option. Today, I will be sharing these ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tps of salt
  • 2 tsp of pepper
  • 13oz Sausage

What to do

  1. Turn on the skillet for eggs
  2. Put 2 Tablespoons of butter in the skillet to melt
  3. Add 2 eggs to skillet
  4. Add 2 tsp of salt
  5. Add 2 tsp of pepper
  6. While eggs cook, turn on second skillet for sausage
  7. Stir the eggs until they begin to scramble
  8. Turn sausages over until they are brown on both sides
  9. Reduce heat, Plate and Serve
